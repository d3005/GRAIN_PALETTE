About GrainPalette

Advanced AI-Powered Rice Classification System

Project Overview

GrainPalette is a cutting-edge deep learning solution that revolutionizes rice grain classification through the power of artificial intelligence and computer vision.

Built using state-of-the-art transfer learning techniques with MobileNetV2, our system provides farmers, researchers, and agricultural enthusiasts with instant, accurate rice variety identification and tailored cultivation recommendations.
